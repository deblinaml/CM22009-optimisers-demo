📐 Algorithm Update Rules

Gradient Descent

φ ← φ - α·∇L

Simple: move opposite to gradient. Can oscillate in narrow valleys and get stuck in saddle points.

Momentum

m ← β·m + (1-β)·∇L
φ ← φ - α·m

Accumulates velocity in consistent directions. Smooths oscillations and can escape shallow local minima.

Adam

m ← β₁·m + (1-β₁)·∇L
v ← β₂·v + (1-β₂)·∇L²
φ ← φ - α·m̂/√(v̂+ε)

Adaptive learning rates per parameter. Combines momentum with RMSprop. Robust default choice.
